Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is PKXD Trading Hub?
Ans: PKXD Trading Hub is a community Discord server where PK XD players can trade items and connect with other traders.
2. Is this server officially affiliated with PK XD?
Ans: No. PKXD Trading Hub is a fan-made community and is not officially affiliated with PK XD or its developers.
3. How do I start trading?
Ans: Go to the designated trading channels, post what you are offering and what you are looking for, and wait for responses. Make sure you follow the trading format and server rules.
4. Are middlemen allowed?
Ans: No. Middlemen are strictly not allowed in this server. All trades are done at your own risk.
5. Does this server have trusted traders?
Ans: No. There are no “trusted trader” roles in this server. Always trade carefully and at your own responsibility.
6. How do I report a scammer?
Ans: Open a ticket in the support channel and provide full proof, including screenshots of the agreement and conversation. Staff will review the case.
7. What proof is required for a scam report?
Ans: You must provide clear, uncropped screenshots showing the full conversation and trade agreement. Edited or incomplete proof may not be accepted.
8. Can I trade real money for in-game items?
Ans: Real money trading is not allowed unless clearly stated in the server rules. Violations may result in punishment.
9. How can I become a staff member?
Ans: Staff applications are handled in the designated staff application channel. Follow the instructions posted there to apply.
10. Why was I warned or muted?
Ans: You may have violated server rules, posted in the wrong channel, or engaged in inappropriate behavior. Please review the rules before contacting staff.
